## Features

- Pokedex ‚Üí A list of all **Pokemons** from the 1<sup>st</sup> Generation to the latest with Pagination
  - `Details` : Shows stats about **Pokemons** such as `attack`, `speed`, `health`, `height`, etc
  - `Nav Bar` : Enter the name or the number of a **Pokemons** in order to find It

- User Account :accessibility: ‚Üí You will be able to create an account with an username and a password. This gives the possibility to get 4 **Pokemons** randomly
  - `PokeAvatar` : Every users have a PokeAvatar that is given randomly and which will be the avatar of the account. This can't be modified.
  - `PokeCoins` : Earn 4 _PokeCoins_ at the creation of your account. One _PokeCoin_ gives you the chance to get 1 **Pokemons** randomly
  - `Shiny ‚ú®` : **Pokemons** can be into their Shiny's form (only **Pokemons** that have Shiny's form)
  - `Mega-Evolution` : **Pokemons** can Mega-Evolve which causes stats boosts
    
- Battle Page üÜö ‚Üí Once you have at least 4 **Pokemons**, you can go fight with other users
  - `Team ‚öîÔ∏è` : Your team Fight has to have 4 **Pokemons**
  - `Earn ü•á` : Every win you earn 1 _PokeCoin_ you can use to get a new **Pokemon**
